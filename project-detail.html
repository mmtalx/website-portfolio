The Title Of The Project

Lighting, Animation, Rendering

This project was a collaborative destruction simulation produced for the Studio name as part of a feature film pipeline. The work involved full environment simulation, rigid-body dynamics, and pyro-based fire and smoke effects rendered in Karma XPU.

The brief called for a sequence where a mid-century brutalist structure collapses under seismic load — requiring both physically accurate debris trajectories and art-directed hero pieces that read clearly on screen. Fracture networks were authored in Houdini's constraint network system and cached as USD for cross-department handoff.

Final comp was handled by the studio's compositing team. Esra's scope covered FX development, sim iteration, look development of the dust and debris materials, and delivery of rendered EXR sequences across twelve shots.
